    • Tax Planning & Strategy

      Reducing tax liabilities requires on-going strategic planning, experience and knowledge. We work in the present while planning for the future.

      Tax is not just about compliance. Tax planning can help to ease your tax burden and allow you to build and distribute wealth. With careful tax planning, you will find yourself in the best tax position you could be. We will ensure you only pay as much tax as you have to. Reducing your company's taxes in today’s environment requires not only intelligent tax planning, but also a commitment to professional excellence and comprehensive service, with deep understanding of your situation and financial goals. We offer service tailored to your needs.

    • Tax Audit Representation and Defence

      Our services are designed to meet your tax compliance and advisory needs. Given the complexities of Japanese Tax Laws, coupled with the aggressive approach of tax auditors, having knowledgeable and experienced professionals to represent you to protect your interests is essential when your company is facing a tax audit.

      You should always ask yourself if your current representative, whether they are an outside consultant or someone from within your company, specializes in dealing with tax laws, regulations, interpretations, policies, and procedures.

  • Accounting Service

    Our accounting team is able to process your accounts, offer timely management advice, and issue periodical (monthly/quarterly/annual) reports. The regulatory burden is increasing, but the human resources to manage it are not. It is important for the tax function to be able to deliver value cost effectively. With our service, you can be freed to focus on core business priorities. We can handle your accounting work on a monthly, quarterly or yearly basis.

     There are three categories of benefits of
    accounting outsourced:

    • Cost cutting

      Cost savings can be achieved on accounting staff salaries, benefits, management, recruitment, training, accommodation, equipment and other capital expenditures.

    • Risk Transfer
